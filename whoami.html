About Me

As a pentester with five years of military network security experience, multiple certifications in the field of cybersecurity, and an Associates degree in Computer Technology, I possess a unique skill set that combines technical expertise with a commitment to excellence. I am a hard-working professional who is passionate about identifying vulnerabilities and strengthening cybersecurity defenses to protect sensitive information and systems.

With a proven track record of success in the military and a strong foundation in industry-standard cybersecurity tools and techniques, I am well-positioned to excel as a pentester. My certifications include, to name a few of them, the OSCP, CRTP, eCPPTv2, eJPT, Pentest+, and Security+, which demonstrate my expertise in cybersecurity and penetration testing. I continue to hone my skills through CTFs, as well as content creation and contributions to projects which can be found on my GitHub, YouTube, and website.

I am adept at working with teams of various sizes and skill levels, and I have experience leading projects and delegating tasks to ensure timely completion. Additionally, my military background has instilled in me a strong sense of discipline, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence that I will bring to every aspect of my work in cybersecurity.

Overall, I am a highly motivated and skilled individual with a passion for cybersecurity and a commitment to continuous learning and improvement. I am excited to leverage my skills and experience to help organizations protect their critical assets and stay ahead of evolving cyber threats.
